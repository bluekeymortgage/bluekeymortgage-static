## What This Page Provides
- **General information** about different senior living options available in Ontario
- **Educational content** to help you understand terminology and categories

- **Questions to ask** when exploring different living arrangements

## What This Page Does NOT Provide
- **Personal recommendations** about specific retirement communities or facilities
- **Medical or care advice** about which level of care is right for you
- **Endorsements** of any particular senior living provider
- **Legal advice** about contracts or residency agreements

## My Role & Expertise
My professional expertise is in **mortgage and home equity solutions** for seniors. I can help you understand:
- How to finance a move to a retirement community
- How to use home equity to fund care services
- Reverse mortgages and refinancing options
- Mortgage solutions for downsizing or rightsizing

## Recommended Next Steps
1. **Consult with healthcare professionals** about your care needs
2. **Visit retirement communities** in person to get a firsthand feel
3. **Talk with family** about preferences and priorities
4. **Consult with a senior living advisor** (many offer free consultation services)
5. **Review contracts carefully** with a lawyer before signing

## Why This Information Is Here
Many of my mortgage clients are exploring senior living options while also considering their financial situation. I provide this information to help you have more informed conversations with the appropriate professionals.

## Always Seek Appropriate Advice
**For senior living decisions:** Consult with geriatric care managers, senior living advisors, or healthcare professionals.

**For financial decisions related to senior living:** I can help with mortgage and home equity questions as part of your overall planning.
