What is Mortgage Refinancing?

Refinancing means replacing your existing mortgage with a new one, typically with different terms and conditions. This can help you access equity, lower your interest rate, change your amortization period, or consolidate debt.

When to refinance:

  • Interest rates have dropped
  • You want to access equity
  • You want to shorten your mortgage term
  • You want to switch from variable to fixed rate

Refinancing benefits:

  • Lower monthly payments
  • Access cash for renovations or expenses
  • Consolidate debts
  • Better rate certainty
