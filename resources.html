Ontario & Canadian Government Programs

Official programs and benefits available to Ontario seniors.

Canada Pension Plan (CPP)

Monthly retirement pension for Canadians who contributed.

Visit CPP Website

Old Age Security (OAS)

Monthly payment for Canadians 65+ who meet residency requirements.

Visit OAS Website

Guaranteed Income Supplement (GIS)

Additional monthly benefit for low-income OAS recipients.

Visit GIS Website

Ontario Drug Benefit Program

Prescription drug coverage for Ontario seniors.
