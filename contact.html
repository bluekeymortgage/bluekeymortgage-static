Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about getting in touch and what to expect.

What happens after I contact you?

I'll respond within 24 hours to acknowledge your message. We can then schedule a free, no-obligation consultation at your convenience—either by phone, video call, or email exchange, whichever you prefer.

Is there a cost for the initial consultation?

No, the initial consultation is completely free and carries no obligation. It's simply a chance for us to get to know each other and for me to understand your situation.

What information should I have ready?

For mortgage-specific questions, it helps to know your approximate home value, any existing mortgage balance, and your age. But don't worry if you don't have all the details—we can start with general information.

Can my adult children be involved in the conversation?

Absolutely. Many seniors prefer to include their children in financial discussions. We can arrange a three-way call or include them in emails if you'd like.

What if I'm not ready to make any decisions yet?

That's perfectly fine. Many people contact me just to gather information and understand their options. There's no pressure to move forward until you're ready.
