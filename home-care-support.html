Companion Care

Home Modifications for Aging in Place

Creating a safe, accessible home environment helps seniors stay independent.

Common Accessibility Modifications

  • Entryways: Ramps, wider doors, automatic door openers
  • Bathrooms: Grab bars, shower seats, raised toilets, non-slip flooring
  • Kitchens: Lowered countertops, accessible appliances, pull-out shelves
  • Bedrooms: Adjustable beds, bedside rails, adequate lighting
  • General: Improved lighting, non-slip flooring, stair lifts, handrails
  • Safety: Emergency response systems, fire safety upgrades

Financing Options: Reverse mortgages, government grants, home equity loans
