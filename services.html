How I'm Compensated

Important information about how my services are funded.

No Direct Costs to Seniors

Important: I do not charge Ontario seniors directly for my consultations, guidance, or resource connections.

Reverse Mortgage Services

I'm compensated by lenders when clients choose a reverse mortgage product that fits their needs.

Referral Services

When I refer clients to trusted partners (attorneys, financial planners, home care providers), those partners may compensate me for qualified referrals.

Information & Guidance

General information, retirement planning advice, and Ontario resource guidance is always provided free of charge.
