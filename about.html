My Story & Mission

Why I'm passionate about helping Ontario seniors navigate their financial decisions.

From Financial Professional to Senior Advocate

With years of experience in the mortgage industry, I noticed a gap: many seniors were receiving generic financial advice that didn't address their unique needs and concerns. They were often overwhelmed by complex terminology and pressured into decisions that didn't feel right.

I decided to focus exclusively on serving homeowners 55+ in Ontario. My goal is to provide the kind of guidance I'd want for my own parents: clear, patient, and focused on their wellbeing—not just the numbers.

Why Seniors Deserve Better

Seniors face unique financial challenges:

  • Fixed incomes that need to stretch further
  • Concerns about preserving home equity for heirs
  • Need for home modifications or care as they age
  • Desire to stay in their homes and communities
  • Confusion about government benefits and programs

These aren't just financial issues—they're deeply personal. That's why I take a holistic approach, considering your entire situation, not just your mortgage.
