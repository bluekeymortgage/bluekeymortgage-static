About Ragini

Your trusted mortgage advisor with a passion for helping homeowners achieve their goals

Meet Ragini

With over 15 years of experience in the mortgage industry, Ragini has helped thousands of Ontarians navigate their mortgage journey. Her passion is making complex financial concepts simple and accessible.

Ragini specializes in helping seniors unlock home equity through reverse mortgages, assisting first-time buyers in achieving homeownership, and providing refinancing strategies that save families money. She believes every homeowner deserves personalized, transparent advice without pressure or hidden fees.

When she's not helping clients, Ragini enjoys volunteering in her community and mentoring young professionals in the mortgage industry.
